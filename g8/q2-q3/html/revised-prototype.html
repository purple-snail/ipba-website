After we analysed the responses, we concluded that the overall responses were positive. But there were still improvements to be made since some of the responses suggested some things that we can add to the prototype. Those suggestions are adding more platforms to upload the podcast to, and giving an example podcast for the people to listen to. So we started to plan and work out what to do. We started implementing more platforms notably YouTube since it was the most requested. After we implemented that, we started planning when to record the podcast since that was one of the suggestions. We also started planning when to record the slide presentation. We decided to record the podcast on the same day but was moved to the next day because we were not complete for recording. Then the next day comes and we weren't able to record again because something came up with one of our members, so we decided to move it again to Friday. On friday, we were able to record the podcast and the slide presentation but the recording for both the podcast and the presentation were both lagging. For the podcast we can just remove the visuals but for the presentation, we needed to redo the recording and we planned to record on monday. When monday arrived, we started to record the presentation on the am session because we just had to finish missing lms tasks in MAPE, which most of us are already done in. After sometime and a few takes, we were able to record the presentation properly.
