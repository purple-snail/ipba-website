We brainstormed what platform we were going to upload the podcast to and what type of podcast we were going to make. Then we got stuck with the type of prototype we were going to make. We just couldn't decide, because we were trying to think on how we can make it and how we can explain it. But after some time me and my group decided to make a digital prototype. Because we were able to figure out how to make and explain it. Then when we were told to create the prototype, we started to make the prototype that we have planned. But when miss came to our gmeet and saw what we were making, she said that we were doing it all wrong and it took some time for her to explain what was wrong and what were we supposed to make because we didn't understand what she was saying.
