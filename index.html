IPBA

Chosen Category
  - Educational Videos
Target audience
  - Teens: We chose teens because they can be influenced by the situation and help spread awareness about the situation in Sitio Bebe.
  - Adults: We chose adults because they can donate or help Sitio Bebe in their current situation.
Needs
  - Teens watch these videos because they want to learn more about a certain topic.
  - Adults watch these types of videos because it helps them learn more about what’s happening in our world.
  - Since the brain responds to visuals faster, teens and adults process the information faster in videos than reading it from text.
  - The adults and teens are curious about how these less fortunate communities handle the problems they are currently facing and how the viewers can help these types of communities.
  - Teens and adults watch these generally for new information.
  Our group chose educational videos in response to the need of the audience to get more information about different topics, what’s happening around the world, and be aware of social problems. This type of content can present alarming issues that they can be aware of, for example, Sitio Bebe (which is a less fortunate community) is currently facing a shortage of clean water, a lack of resources, and the inability to access medical healthcare, so by watching an educational video they can learn about the situation of the community and how they can assist them.
