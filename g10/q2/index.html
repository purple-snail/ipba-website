The upcoming podcast series, "The Effects of Gaming on the Academic Performance of Junior High School Students," will explore the relationship between gaming and academic achievement. The series will analyze gaming habits, including time management, cognitive skills development, and game genres. A guest from Apec Kalumpang will provide firsthand insights into the gaming culture within the school community. The podcast aims to encourage critical thinking and informed decision-making among students, promoting conscious choices about gaming habits and recognizing its potential positive contributions to personal development. The series will be engaging and informative.

According to (Basrizal 2023), overindulgence in gaming may hurt academic performance. Studies show that learners who spend much time playing slots tend to perform below average in school. Gaming addiction results in a lack of motivation and ineffective time management. These cases have an impact on academic achievement.

Based on the survey that we conducted in the Junior high school, the attitude of some individuals may hold the belief that gaming has a negative impact on academic performance due to potential time distraction, while others may have a more neutral stance, seeing gaming as a form of entertainment that can be managed responsibly.

There could also be people who believe that gaming can have positive effects, such as enhancing problem-solving skills and teamwork.
